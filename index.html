Lizzie Bentley – Stratford-upon-Avon, UK

About

Lizzie Bentley is an oil painter (working from life) and a printmaker based in Stratford-upon-Avon, UK [oai_citation:14‡lizziebentley.com](http://lizziebentley.com/about-1#:~:text=Lizzie%20Bentley%20is%20an%20Oil,and%20a%20Printmaker). Born in Birmingham, she studied Fine Art at the Ruskin School of Art (Oxford University) and continued her training at Heatherley’s School of Art, the Prince’s Drawing School, and a year of studio practice in Kew with Jason Bowyer [oai_citation:15‡lizziebentley.com](http://lizziebentley.com/about-1#:~:text=Born%20in%20Birmingham%2C%20Lizzie%20studied,Practice%20with%20Jason%20Bowyer%20NEAC). She combines traditional painting skills with over 20 years of digital art experience in the film and TV industry. Lizzie exhibits regularly and is a member of The Gallery at the Guild in Chipping Campden, where some of her paintings are permanently on display [oai_citation:16‡lizziebentley.com](http://lizziebentley.com/about-1#:~:text=Lizzie%20is%20a%20permanent%20member,her%20paintings%20plus%20browser%20pieces).

In addition to painting, Lizzie has an extensive career as a digital matte painter and concept artist for film/TV, with credits including *Game of Thrones* (Season 1) among many others [oai_citation:17‡lizziebentley.com](http://lizziebentley.com/about-1#:~:text=Lizzie%20has%20traditional%20painting%20skills,CV%20tab%20for%20her%20credits). (See the CV section below for a full list of credits.) She is available for contract work and also offers art classes. Lizzie’s work can be purchased through local galleries or directly from her studio [oai_citation:18‡lizziebentley.com](http://lizziebentley.com/about-1#:~:text=Lizzie%20is%20available%20for%20contract,prints%20I%20have%20done%20recently).

Printmaking

Lizzie creates bespoke prints and illustrations for individuals and designers, leveraging her 20+ years as a digital matte painter and concept artist [oai_citation:21‡lizziebentley.com](http://lizziebentley.com/new-prints#:~:text=I%20make%20bespoke%20prints%20and,illustrations%20for%20individuals%20and%20Designers). She produces prints in any size (bespoke or standard) on high-quality art paper. All prints are sold directly from her studio, often in limited editions [oai_citation:22‡lizziebentley.com](http://lizziebentley.com/new-prints#:~:text=The%20prints%20are%20for%20sale,run%20for%20the%20below%20prints). A signed A3 print is typically £95, numbered and hand-signed by Lizzie. Please inquire for custom print orders or other sizes.

CV

This section presents Lizzie Bentley’s full curriculum vitae, including her education, affiliations, and a comprehensive list of film and television credits. You can also download a PDF version of the CV.

Education & Training

  • Oxford University (Ruskin School of Art) – MA (Oxon) Fine Art, 1990–1993
  • Heatherley’s School of Art – Courses in Fine Art (2000–2005)
  • Life Painting with Francis Bowyer – Richmond (2007–2009)
  • Studio Practice with Jason Bowyer NEAC – Kew Steam Museum (2016–2017)
  • Additional classes at the Prince’s Drawing School, Slade School of Art (Summer programs), and The London School of Painting and Drawing with Andy Pankhurst.

Professional Membership: Member of the British Film Designers Guild.

Professional Artwork & Teaching (2023–2025)

During 2023–24, with the industry-wide Writers/Actors strike affecting film work, Lizzie provided commissioned artwork to various interior designers. Her artwork is featured in venues such as:

  • The Lainston House (Hotel), Hampshire
  • Hedsor House, Buckinghamshire
  • Sherborne House, Cheltenham
  • Ansty Hall, Coventry
  • Sloane Club, Chelsea

Lizzie also began teaching oil painting, life drawing, and portrait painting classes (see News section for details). She continues to paint and take on new commissions.
