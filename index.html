About

Lizzie Bentley is an oil painter (working from life) and a printmaker based in Stratford-upon-Avon, UK.

Born in Birmingham, she studied Fine Art at the Ruskin School of Art (Oxford University). She continued her training at Heatherley’s School of Art and at the Prince’s Drawing School, and spent a year of studio practice in Kew with Jason Bowyer.

She combines traditional painting skills with over 20 years of digital art experience in the film and TV industry. Lizzie exhibits regularly and is a member of The Gallery at the Guild in Chipping Campden, where some of her paintings are permanently on display.

In addition to painting, Lizzie has an extensive career as a digital matte painter and concept artist for film and TV, with credits including Game of Thrones (Season 1) among many others (see the CV section below for a full list of credits). She is available for contract work and also offers art classes. Lizzie’s work can be purchased through local galleries or directly from her studio.
