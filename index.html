About

Lizzie Bentley studied Fine Art at the Ruskin School of Drawing & Fine Art (Oxford University), with further training at Heatherley’s and the Prince’s Drawing School. She has two decades of experience in digital matte painting and concept artistry for film and TV, and now focuses on oil painting, printmaking, workshops, and bespoke design commissions.
