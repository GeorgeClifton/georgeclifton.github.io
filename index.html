About

Lizzie Bentley is an Oil Painter (who paints from life) and a Printmaker.

She lives in Stratford-upon-Avon, UK. Born in Birmingham, Lizzie studied Fine Art at the Ruskin School of Drawing and Fine Art (Oxford University). Lizzie then attended Heatherley’s School of Art, attended numerous classes at the Prince’s Drawing School and spent a year in Kew doing Studio Practice with Jason Bowyer NEAC.

Lizzie is a permanent member of the Gallery at The Guild in Chipping Campden where you will always find a few of her paintings plus browser pieces.
Address: The Old Silk Mill, Sheep Street, Chipping Campden, Gloucestershire GL56 9SQ (Open everyday 10am to 4pm.)

Lizzie has traditional painting skills and also digital skills (with over 20 years’ experience painting Digital Matte Paintings and Concept Paintings for the film and TV industry. She has a long list of film and TV credits to her name including Game of Thrones Season 1! See the CV section for her credits).

Lizzie is available for contract work for designers. Please check out some prints she has done recently.

She exhibits regularly and you can buy her work at The Gallery at the Guild (Chipping Campden) and The White Gallery (Chipping Campden), or direct from her studio.
